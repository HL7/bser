History

This specification was first balloted in September 2018. The ballot failed for two primary reasons -

  1. the broken hyperlinks prevented balloters from navigating the specification
  2. The guide neglected to state any workflow support

Each of these issues have been adressed in this version of the guide.

The full ballot reconciliation spreadsheet has been posted to the September ballot site http://bit.ly/2TLCcnC and is being tracked on gForge http://bit.ly/2Y5dJce.
